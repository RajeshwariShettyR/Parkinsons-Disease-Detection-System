About Parkinson's Disease

Parkinson's Disease is a neurodegenerative disorder that affects movement. It occurs when nerve cells in the brain become damaged or die, leading to symptoms like tremors, stiffness, and slowness of movement. Early detection and intervention can lead to better management and improved quality of life for individuals living with the disease.
